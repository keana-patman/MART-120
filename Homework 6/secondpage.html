Summary of a Sad Story


There was one point in my life where I actually read books. The one book that has made my cry the most was:

Where the Red Fern Grows By Wilson Rawls

Here is a Summary of the book if you are interested.

It is the story of Billy, a poor farm boy growing up in the Ozarks in Oklahoma,
who works to buy and train two coonhound hunting dogs, Old Dan and Little Ann.
Together with his dogs, Billy becomes a champion coon hunter and they go on some fun and some tragic adventures together.
Sadly, one thing leads to another and Billy might have to leave the Ozarks to a place where the dogs won't be happy.
However, as fate has it, it's a sign from God that decides their futures.
Where the Red Fern Grows is a classic coming-of-age story.

I would highly recommend the book.
